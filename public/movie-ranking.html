My top 3 movie of all-time

Howl’s Moving Castle

I love the quirky magic and how emotional the story is without being over the top.

My Neighbor Totoro

It’s just pure joy and nostalgia; it makes me feel like a kid again.

Princess Mononoke

The way it shows the struggle between nature and humans is so intense and beautiful.
